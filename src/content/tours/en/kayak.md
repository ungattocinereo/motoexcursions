## Tour overview

🔵 Kayaking along the most beautiful coastline of Naples. You need absolutely no experience — the boats are stable, wide, strong, and light. Even kids handle them.

We paddle along the shore of one of the greenest, most scenic parts of the city. Clean emerald sea, stunning panoramas, quiet coves. We stop at the best spots for swimming, lunch, or just to explore — for example, sea caves or photogenic corners where you'll get plenty of great shots.

The day is easy and relaxed. You'll see a Naples that will genuinely surprise you. There are easier routes and more advanced ones with caves and richer stops.

## What is included

- Kayak / canoe and basic equipment
- Experienced guide alongside you
- Choice of route level (easy or advanced)

## What is not included

- Personal insurance
- Food and drinks
- Sunscreen and water shoes

## Important conditions

- Sunscreen is essential.
- Bring a cap or hat and sunglasses.
- Water shoes are required — we walk on land, volcanic rocky shore, and in caves.
- Bring a snack for lunch and some water.
