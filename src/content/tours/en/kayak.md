## Tour overview

Kayaking along the most beautiful coastline of Naples. You need absolutely no experience — the boats are stable, wide, strong, and light. Even kids handle them.

We paddle along the shore of one of the greenest, most scenic parts of the city. Clean emerald sea, stunning panoramas, quiet coves. We stop at the best spots for swimming, lunch, or just to explore — for example, sea caves or photogenic corners where you will get plenty of great shots.

The number of people can be from 1 to any group size. The price changes depending on the number of people: €50 per person starts for groups of 5 people.

There is also a personal option for one person as a passenger in the kayak — €250. I personally steer the kayak; you do not paddle and simply enjoy the views.

## What is included

- Kayak / canoe and basic equipment
- Experienced guide alongside you
- Choice of route level: easy or advanced
- Stops for swimming, photos, and coastline exploration

## What is not included

- Personal insurance
- Food and drinks

## Important conditions

- Duration: full day.
- Price from €50 per person for groups of 5 people.
- The personal passenger-kayak tour is available only for one person and costs €250.
- Sunscreen is essential.
- Bring a cap or hat and sunglasses.
- Water shoes are required — we walk on land, volcanic rocky shore, and in caves.
- Bring a snack for lunch and some water.
