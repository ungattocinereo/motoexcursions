## Tour overview

If you're confident on a bike, you can see so much more — places you simply can't reach on foot, because they're too far out and not known.

I'll show you the best panoramas and the real character of the city, guide you through plenty of non-touristy spots, and we'll have lunch with the locals. The route can be adapted to your level and pace.

## What is included

- Guide who knows the best locations
- Route planning tailored to your level
- Photo and panoramic stops

## What is not included

- Bicycle and helmet (rental can be arranged on request)
- Food and drinks
- Personal insurance

## Important conditions

- Confident bike-handling skills required.
- Closed, comfortable shoes.
- Bring water and a snack.
- Duration ranges from a few hours up to a full day on request.
