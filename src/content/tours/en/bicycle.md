## Tour overview

If you are confident on a bike, you can see so much more — places you simply cannot reach on foot because they are too far out and not widely known.

I will show you the best panoramas and the real character of the city, guiding you through many non-touristy places. The route can be adapted to your level and pace.

## What is included

- Guide who knows the best locations
- Route planning tailored to your level
- Photo and panoramic stops

## What is not included

- Bicycle and helmet; rental can be arranged on request
- Food and drinks
- Personal insurance

## Important conditions

- Duration: from 4 hours.
- Confident bike-handling skills required.
- Closed, comfortable shoes.
- Bring water and a snack.
